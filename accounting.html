Getting started

First, we get you current.

Almost every creator comes to us with some catching up to do: a few months behind, or a year of brand deals run through a personal card. Before monthly work starts, your bookkeeper cleans up the foundation. It's a one-time fee, quoted up front.

Setup

Current books

Already roughly up to date? We connect your accounts, set your chart of accounts, and take it from there. One-time setup, starting at $300.

Catch-up

A few months behind

We rebuild and reconcile each month that's missing, priced per month of catch-up, so you only pay for the ground we actually cover.

Rebuild

A year (or more) of mess

Commingled, unreconciled, or never really kept? We rebuild the whole stretch into clean, tax-ready books. Quoted after a quick look.

Catch-up and onboarding are billed once, separately from your monthly plan, and scale with how many months need rebuilding, so tidy books cost less and a long backlog costs more. You'll always get the number before any work begins.

Add-ons

Build on top of any plan.

Bolt on what you need, when you need it. Priced per project or per month, quoted up front, never buried.
